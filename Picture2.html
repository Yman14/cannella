CUMIN

Cumin is a flowering plant in the family Apiaceae, native from the east Mediterranean to South Asia. Its seeds ( each one contained within a fruit, which is dried) are used in the cuisines of many different cultures, in both whole and ground form. It also has many uses as a traditional medicinal plant.


Benefits of Cumin:

  • Has Anticancer Properties
  • May Help Treat Diarrhea
  • Helps Control Blood Sugar
  • Fights Bacteria And Parasites
  • May Protect Against Cancer
  • Boosts Memory
  • Has An Anti-Inflammatory Effect
  • Aids In Weight Loss

Cumin has a variety of benefits ranging from helping improve your digestion to boosting your memory. Cumin may also prevent cancer cells from multiplying and help reduce pain with its anti-inflammatory properties.
